# Product Vision

## Vision Statement

To transform traditional software metrics dashboards into an AI-powered delivery intelligence experience that enables:

Teams and leaders to interact with metrics in natural language
Early prediction of delivery risks
Faster root cause discovery
Proactive decision-making with confidence

## Problem We Are Solving

Software delivery data is often available, but not easily actionable.

Current challenges include:

Manual navigation across dashboards, teams, and metrics
Delayed identification of delivery risks and bottlenecks
Limited ability to predict outcomes before issues escalate
High effort required to interpret trends and investigate causes
Reactive decision-making driven by static, historical reporting

## Target Users

### Engineering Managers
Need to monitor team delivery health, identify bottlenecks, and intervene early.

### Delivery Leads / TPMs
Need to track predictability, dependencies, flow efficiency, and cross-team risks.

### Executives / Senior Leaders
Need concise portfolio-level insights, trends, risks, and recommended actions.

### Teams / Scrum Masters
Need to understand flow, quality, and operational performance to improve delivery.
